# Speedy Cheetahs — Coach Playbook

Welcome to the Speedy Cheetahs team portal! This guide covers everything you need to know as a coach.

---

## Getting Started

### Login

1. Go to **https://speedy-cheetahs-production.up.railway.app/login**
2. Enter the email and password provided by the Head Coach
3. You'll land on the **Dashboard** — your home base

### Forgot Your Password?

Click **"Forgot your password?"** on the login page, enter your email, and you'll receive a reset link (valid for 15 minutes). You can also ask the Head Coach to reset it for you.

---

## What You Can Do

### Dashboard

Your at-a-glance overview:

- **Total Players** — quick count with a link to the full roster
- **Upcoming Events** — next 5 games and practices
- **Recent Updates** — latest announcements
- **Game Day** — jump straight into live game tracking

---

### Schedule

View and manage all team events (practices, games, and other events).

**To create an event:**
1. Click **"+ Add Event"**
2. Choose type: Practice, Game, or Other
3. Fill in date/time, location, and any notes
4. Click **Create**

Events automatically show up on the public schedule for parents to see. You can edit, cancel, or delete events at any time.

---

### Game Day

Track attendance, batting, and fielding in real time during games.

**How to use it:**
1. Go to **Game Day** and select the game
2. **Attendance tab** — tap each player to mark Present or Absent
3. **Batting tab** — set the lineup order and track at-bats
4. **Fielding tab** — assign field positions to players

Everything syncs live, so multiple coaches can update at the same time.

---

### Updates & Announcements

Post announcements that appear on the home page for all parents.

**To post an update:**
1. Go to **Updates**
2. Click **"+ Post Update"**
3. Enter a title and message
4. Optionally check **SMS** and/or **Email** to notify parents instantly
5. Click **Post**

Only families who have opted in to notifications will receive SMS or email. After posting, you can **edit** or **delete** your own updates using the pencil and trash icons. The Head Coach can edit or delete any update.

---

### Coaching Insights — Ask AI

Get instant coaching advice powered by AI.

**How to use it:**
1. Go to **Coaching**
2. Type any question in the text box — for example:
   - *"Create a 60-minute practice plan focused on hitting fundamentals"*
   - *"What drills work best for kids afraid of the ball?"*
   - *"How do I teach proper throwing mechanics to 7-year-olds?"*
3. Click **Ask AI** and wait a few seconds for a response

All your previous questions and answers are saved so you can refer back to them anytime.

---

### Rules & Knowledge Base

A reference hub for league rules and coaching resources.

- **Rules Editor** — The Head Coach posts the official Farm-1 league rules here
- **Ask AI** — Type any rules question and get an instant answer based on the saved rules
- **Knowledge Base** — Add extra resources (articles, PDFs, or web links) that the AI will reference when answering questions

---

### Roster

View all players with jersey numbers, family contacts, and medical/allergy notes.

The Head Coach manages adding and editing players. All coaches can view the full roster with parent contact info.

---

### Volunteer Management

Organize parent volunteers for games and practices.

**To create volunteer roles:**
1. Go to **Volunteer**
2. Select an event
3. Add roles (Scoreboard, Equipment Manager, etc.) or use the quick-add templates
4. Set how many volunteers are needed per role

Parents sign up on their own through the public Volunteer page. You'll see who signed up and how many spots are still open.

---

### Settings

- **Change your password** — available to all coaches
- **Manage assistant coaches** — Head Coach only (add, edit, reset passwords, remove)
- **Shareable links** — direct URLs you can text to parents for Schedule, Volunteer, Updates, and Registration
