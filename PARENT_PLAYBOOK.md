# Speedy Cheetahs — Parent & Family Guide

Welcome to the Speedy Cheetahs team portal! This guide covers everything you need to know as a parent or family member.

**No login required** — all parent features are open and accessible from your phone or computer.

---

## Team Portal

**https://speedy-cheetahs-production.up.railway.app**

Bookmark this link or add it to your home screen for quick access.

---

## What You Can Do

### Home Page

Your starting point — shows:

- **Upcoming games and practices** (next 5 events)
- **Recent announcements** from coaches
- Quick-access tiles to all parent features

---

### View Schedule

See all upcoming games, practices, and team events.

- **Game days** show the opponent name and location
- **Practices** show time, location, and any coach notes
- Cancelled events are clearly marked
- Dates, times, and locations are all listed so you can plan ahead

**Direct link:** https://speedy-cheetahs-production.up.railway.app/schedule

---

### Updates & Announcements

Stay in the loop with coach announcements.

- All team updates are posted here with timestamps
- Important news, schedule changes, reminders, and more
- Updates also appear on the home page

**Direct link:** https://speedy-cheetahs-production.up.railway.app/updates

---

### Parent Registration

This is where you manage your family's contact information and notification preferences.

**How to use it:**

1. Go to **Parent Registration**
2. Find your child's name in the roster (organized in two columns)
3. Tap their name to expand the registration form
4. Fill in or update:
   - **Parent/Guardian name**
   - **Email address**
   - **Phone number**
   - **SMS notifications** — check this to receive text message alerts from coaches
   - **Email notifications** — check this to receive email alerts from coaches
5. Click **Save**

**Have multiple kids on the team?** Siblings are linked automatically — update your info once and it applies to all your children.

**Additional contacts:** You can add emergency contacts or a second parent/guardian with their own name, phone, email, and relationship.

**Direct link:** https://speedy-cheetahs-production.up.railway.app/register

---

### Volunteer Sign-Up

Help out at games and practices! Coaches post volunteer roles and you can sign up right from your phone.

**How to sign up:**

1. Go to **Volunteer**
2. Browse open roles for upcoming events (e.g., Scoreboard, Equipment Manager, Line-Up Card)
3. See how many spots are still available
4. Enter your name and phone number
5. Click **Sign Up**

Your info is saved so you don't have to re-enter it next time. Filled roles are grayed out so you can see what's still needed.

**Direct link:** https://speedy-cheetahs-production.up.railway.app/volunteer

## Staying Notified

### Text Messages (SMS)

If you opt in during registration, you'll receive text alerts when coaches post important updates. Messages come from the team's notification number with a **[Speedy Cheetahs]** tag.

### Email

If you opt in during registration, you'll receive email notifications for coach announcements. Emails include team branding and the update content.

### How to Opt In or Out

1. Go to **Parent Registration**
2. Find and tap your child's name
3. Check or uncheck the **SMS** and **Email** notification boxes
4. Click **Save**

You can change your preferences at any time.
